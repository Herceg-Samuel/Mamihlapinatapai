<Title>
  Have you ever looked at a clock at <Date>2:00 AM</Date> and realized that you
  haven't actually built anything meaningful in the last six months?
</Title>
<Pause></Pause>
The Germans have a word for this. **Torschlusspanik**.

It literally translates
to <Marker>"gate-closing panic."</Marker>

<Pause variant="moon">
  It's the anxiety that time is running out to achieve your goals, find a
  partner, or figure out what the hell you are doing with your life.
</Pause>

<Caption>The Mathematics of Panic</Caption>

When I try to map this feeling out, it usually looks like an inverse
relationship between _time remaining_ and _expectations_.

<Pronunciation>

<Meaning>
