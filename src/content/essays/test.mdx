Have you ever looked at a clock at 2:00 AM and realized that you haven't actually built anything meaningful in the last six months?

The Germans have a word for this. **Torschlusspanik**. It literally translates to "gate-closing panic." It's the anxiety that time is running out to achieve your goals, find a partner, or figure out what the hell you are doing with your life.
