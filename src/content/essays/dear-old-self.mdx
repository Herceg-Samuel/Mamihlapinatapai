<Title>DEAR OLD SELF</Title>

<Pause />

I SAID TO DEAR OLD SELF THAT BY WRITING YOU CAN FINALLY FEEL ALL THOSE EMOTIONS YOU FLED. INTELLECTUALIZE EVERYTHING. THE CONSTANT BATTLES YOU FIGHT EVEN BEFORE YOU PUT YOUR LEGS ON THE COLD CRUEL FLOOR EARLY IN THE GOD-DAMN MORNING.

<Pause />

THE NIGHTMARE YOU BATTLE IN YOUR SLEEP AND THE MOSTERS YOU FACE WHEN YOU START TO LOOSE SLEEP. THE BEHEMOTH THAT STARES RIGHT INTO YOUR LOST EYES JUST WHEN YOU GET TO SEE THE LIGHT OF DAY. NO. RIGHT STARES DEEP INTO YOUR VERY HEART AND SOUL.

<Pause />

SOMETIMES IT WHISPERS SO ONLY YOU CAN HEAR IT. OTHER TIMES IT SCREAMS LOUD ENOUGH TO MAKE YOU REALISE THIS IS YOUR PRESENT REALITY. LOCKED INSIDE THIS CAGE CALLED REALITY. MOSTLY IT LAUGHS TO YOUR FACE.

<Quote>
> " OOH THE BOY CAN DREAM. A BETTER FUTURE. WHO DOES HE THINK HE IS? TO CHASE AFTER A LIFE!. TO LOOK FOR WHAT HE WANTS. WHAT THE HELL DOES HE EVEN WANT? HE IS CRAZY RIGHT?. HE WANTS SOMEONE TO FINALLY SEE HIM. NO MORE MASKS. ISN'T HE THE SAME GUY THAT WAKES UP AND CHOSES WHAT MASKS SUITS THE DAY.

> "IS IT THE BLUE ONE THAT I LOVE, THE RED THAT HELPS ME FOCUS." HE TREASURES THEM AND CLEANS THEM EVERYDAY. MAKES SURE THEY ARE NOT STOLEN AND TAKEN AWAY FROM HIM. "

</Quote>

<Pause />

MOSTLY THEY ALL GATHER AND ATTACK IN UNISON. THE NIGHTMARE, THE MONSTER AND THE BEHEMOTH. THEY SAY

<Quote>
> " WHY NOT?

> THIS IS SO BLOODY FUN. DID YOU SEE HIS FACE WHEN WE AMBUSHED HIM. HE WAKES UP SHOCKED AND SURPRISED AS THOUGH HE IS NOT ACCUSTOMED TO OUR DAILY VISITS. OOH WOW. THIS JUST MAKES OUR DAY. HAVE YOU SEEN HOW HE QUICKLY TAKES ON HIS MASK FROM HIS CLOSET. HE THINKS THAT THEN WE CAN NOT RECOGNISE HIM LIKE ALL THOSE PEOPLE. HOW FUNNY IT IS. WE GAVE HIM ALL THOSE MASKS SO THAT HE CAN FEEL A SENSE OF SAFETY AND WE CAN HAVE THIS FUN DAILY. WE ALSO GET LUCKY HE HAPPENS TO SLEEP DURING THE DAY. WE CEASE THE MOMENT HAVE OUR FUN. OOH WOW. DAMN "

</Quote>

<Pause />

EMOTIONS ARE NOT FOR ME. HELL NO. I CHOOSE TO MAKE SENSE OF THIS BROKEN WORLD OF MINE. INTELLECT BECAME THE LENS THROUGH WHICH I AVOID MY EMOTIONS THAT I COINCIDENTALLY AND SUPPOSSEDLY HAD TO DEAL WITH. WHAT GOOD DOES IT DO TO BOTH KNOW AND FEEL THAT YOU CANNOT HAVE WHAT YOU WANT. AND NO. IT IS NOT FOR SELFISH GAIN. OR IS THAT WHAT I WANT TO CONVINCE MYSELF.

<Pause />

BUT WE BOTH KNOW THAT IS MY BIG WEAKNESS. THE GREATEST FLAW. LYING TO MYSELF. TO TRY AND CONVINCE ME THAT MAYBE IT WILL BE OK. SOMEDAY, RIGHT. RIGHT!!!SOMEBODY. ANYBODY. NO ONE. YEAH I KNOW. AN OPEN MIND BUT TO A KNOWN REALITY. WHAT GOOD IS IT?

<Pause />

A REALISTIC PERSON. THAT MEANS I CHOOSE TO SEE THINGS THE WAY THEY ARE. NOT WHAT I WANT IT TO BE.

<Quote>
> THERE IS A PLACE AND A TIME FOR THAT FANTASY WORLD. NOT HERE AND ASOLUTELY NOT NOW!
</Quote>

<Pause />

I MAY BE WRONG. OPEN TO UNDERSTANDING AND SEEKING WISDOM.

<Pause />

ALRIGHT.
